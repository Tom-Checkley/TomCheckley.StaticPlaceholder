About me

Hi, I'm Tom and I'm living the dream, I get paid for my hobby! I have around 9 years development experience. I currently work on builds and maintenance for a broad range of clients.

I started coding professionally in 2017 at Element 78 as a Frontend Developer, improving the skills I'd been teaching myself whilst coding as a hobby. I love taking the Design and UX Team's ideas to create solutions that makes our clients shine.

Whilst on the Frontend Team, I became more and more involved in the Javascript side of FE development and have grown a real passion for the more programatic side of coding. In 2021 I switched to the Backend Team where I have been developing my skills in C#, .Net and creating great content editor expeiences with Umbraco CMS.

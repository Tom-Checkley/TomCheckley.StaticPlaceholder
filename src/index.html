About me

Hi I'm Tom and I'm living the dream, I get paid for my hobby! I have around about 9 years development experience and I currently work on builds and maintenance for a braod range of clients at Element 78 based in Bristol.

I started coding professionally in 2017 at Element 78 as a Frontend Developer, developing the skills I'd been teaching myself whilst coding as a hobby. I love taking the Design and UX teams ideas to create a solution that makes our clients shine.

Whilst on the Frontend Team I became more and more involved in the Javascript side of FE development and have grown a real passion for the more programatic side of coding working with Angular (js and 2+), Vue and vanilla Javascript.

Due to this, when a position in the Backend Team opened up in 2021 I chucked myself straight back to the beginning on a whole new learning curve and switched teams, learning C#, .Net and creating great content editor user expeiences whith Umbraco CMS.
